Overview

Banking and Insurance Exams are conducted for recruitment in public sector banks and insurance companies. These exams test candidates on quantitative aptitude, reasoning, and general awareness.

IBPS PO and Clerk Exams

The IBPS conducts exams for the recruitment of Probationary Officers and Clerks in public sector banks. The selection process includes a preliminary exam, a main exam, and an interview.

  • Syllabus: Reasoning, Quantitative Aptitude, English Language, General Awareness.
  • Eligibility Criteria: Graduation in any discipline.
  • Pay Scale: INR 23,700 - 42,020 per month.
  • Job Grade: Group A and B.

SBI PO and Clerk Exams

Conducted by the State Bank of India, these exams are highly competitive and require thorough preparation in various subjects including English, Mathematics, and General Knowledge.

  • Syllabus: English Language, Quantitative Aptitude, Reasoning Ability, General Awareness.
  • Eligibility Criteria: Graduation from a recognized university.
  • Pay Scale: INR 27,620 - 50,970 per month.
  • Job Grade:Job Grade: Group A and B.

Insurance Exams

These exams are for various positions in public and private insurance companies. Candidates must prepare for aptitude tests and interviews to secure a position.

  • Syllabus: General Knowledge, Reasoning, and Insurance-related topics.
  • Eligibility Criteria: Graduation in any discipline.
  • Pay Scale: INR 25,000 - 60,000 per month.
  • Job Grade: Varies by position.
